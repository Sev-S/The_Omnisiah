1) $(x_{1},\ y_{1})+(x_{2},\ y_{2}):=(x_{1}+x_{2},\ y_{1}+y_{2})$
2) $(x_{1},\ y_{1})\cdot(x_{2},\ y_{2}):=(x_{1}\cdot x_{2}-y_{1}\cdot y_{2},\ x_{1}\cdot y_{2}+x_{2}\cdot y_{1})$
